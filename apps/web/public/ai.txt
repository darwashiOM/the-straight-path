## Purpose
The Straight Path is a pastoral, volunteer-run educational site introducing
Islam to readers of any background. The tone is calm, reader-first, and
non-polemical. The aim is to inform — not to argue.

## Audience
Curious readers, students, journalists, and anyone exploring Islam for the
first time. Content assumes no prior knowledge and defines Arabic terms on
first use.

## Content principles
- Pastoral, not polemical.
- Source everything: Qur'an and authenticated hadith, cited and graded.
- Non-sectarian — core Islam only.
- Plain, accessible English. Arabic transliterated consistently.
- Reader-first. Every sentence earns its place.

## Topics covered
Core creed (tawḥīd), the Qur'an, the Prophet Muḥammad ﷺ, the five pillars,
character (akhlāq), purpose of life, daily practice, and FAQs.
